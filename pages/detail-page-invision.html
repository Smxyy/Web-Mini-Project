Prototyping Made Easy: Introduction to Invision

Course Description

This course introduces you to Invision, a powerful prototyping tool that helps bring your web and app ideas to life.

What you’ll learn

Create Interactive Prototypes: Craft interactive prototypes that simulate the user experience of your website or app.

Design Mockups and User Interfaces: Build high-fidelity mockups and user interfaces to showcase your design vision.

Collaboration and Feedback: Learn how to share your prototypes with stakeholders and collaborate effectively.

Integration with Design Tools (Optional): Explore how Invision integrates with popular design tools like Figma or Sketch.

By the end of this course, you'll be able to:

Design and build interactive prototypes using Invision's intuitive features.

Communicate your design ideas effectively through high-fidelity mockups and prototypes.

Collaborate with team members and gather valuable feedback on your designs.

This course is ideal for:

Web designers and developers looking to streamline their design and prototyping workflow.

Product managers and UX/UI designers seeking to create interactive prototypes for user testing.

Freelancers and agencies seeking to showcase their design proposals effectively.

Individuals seeking to build basic websites or web applications.
